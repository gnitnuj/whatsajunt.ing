Medewi

A Surf Retreat

A Surf Retreat is something that has been in the works for many years with my sister. The plan is to build a surf retreat located in Medewi, Indonesia, accessible from Bali by car, and the longest wave in Bali (left-break).

Where we're at?

2023 was a big year, we secured a parcel of land in Medewi and found local partners for the future project as we did not want to just build something without input from our future neighbors. Figuring out the process for foreigners to own land in Indonesia was complex and time-consuming, but through sheer perseverance, we were able to make it happen. Definitely send me me a note if you're curious! :)

2024 shaped up to be another exciting year, as we continue to work on the project and explore. We are excited to share more updates with you as we progress.

Where we're going?

I'd like to say that 2025 is the year we get everything built, but let's be real, when has construction ever finished on time? I'll settle for breaking ground. ;)
